TripSapien AI Itinerary Validation Data

Public demonstration rows and canonical pages for reviewing AI-generated travel itineraries against the place, date, booking, neighborhood, and map signals available for matched stops. TripSapien was previously Tripnostic and ValidaTrip, and originally TripPaste.
