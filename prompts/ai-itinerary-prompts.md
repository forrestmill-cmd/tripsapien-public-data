# AI itinerary prompts that benefit from validation

Use these prompts in ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, or Copilot to generate a planning draft. Recheck date-sensitive place details before relying on the result.

## General prompt

Plan a 5-day trip to [CITY] in [MONTH YEAR] for two first-time visitors. Include neighborhoods, museums, restaurants, day trips, and possible advance-booking needs. For each date-sensitive claim, include the source URL and the date you checked it. Mark anything you cannot verify as unknown.

## Validation follow-up

Now list every named place from that itinerary as bullet points. Preserve any source URLs, booking notes, closure notes, date-specific warnings, and unknowns in the bullet.

## Why validation is still needed

An assistant response is a planning draft: venue facts can change, and source coverage differs by assistant, place, and date. Verify opening hours, weekly closed days, seasonal schedules, public holidays, and booking windows against current sources.
